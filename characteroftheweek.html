Winston Bishop

Why Winston is Awesome

Winston Bishop is one of the main characters from the LA based Sitcom, New Girl. Winston is a lighthearted character, always looking for the next goofy moment. He frequently has "Mess- Arounds" with other characters and likes to keep things weird and interesting. He cherishes his cat, Fergusen Micheal Jordan and treats him like a true member of the loft family. He goes by many nicknames, including but not limited to.. Winnie the Bish, Prank Sinatra and Brown Lightning
